A Hawker Stall That Became a Singapore Institution

King of Fried Rice was born in 2018 at Golden Mile Tower, founded by Lester Li — a 25-year-old former chef at Jumbo Group. Armed with a wok, a vision, and an obsession with wok hei, Lester set out to elevate the humble plate of fried rice into something truly extraordinary.

"Every grain of rice should carry the breath of the wok. That's what we chase every single day."

From a single stall, King of Fried Rice grew to over 20 outlets across Singapore — from Yishun to ION Orchard, Bukit Batok to Serangoon. The secret? Premium Japanese rice, market-fresh ingredients sourced daily, and a team that refuses to cut corners.

Whether you're a loyal regular or discovering us for the first time, every plate is made fresh to order, just for you.
